Of course! Here are some more examples of sentences with gerunds:

1. **Running** is good for your health.
   - "Running" is the subject of the sentence.

2. He enjoys **cooking** Italian food.
   - "Cooking" is the object of the verb "enjoys."

3. **Watching** movies is my favorite hobby.
   - "Watching" is the subject of the sentence.

4. They talked about **going** to the beach.
   - "Going" is the object of the preposition "about."
   
5. **Writing** emails takes a lot of time.
   - "Writing" is the subject of the sentence.

6. She is interested in **learning** new languages.
   - "Learning" is the object of the preposition "in."

These examples show how gerunds can be used in different parts of a sentence to act like nouns.
